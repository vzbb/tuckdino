# Product Guidelines

## Visual Style (Aesthetic & UI)
- **Cozy & Warm:** Use a soft, saturated color palette (e.g., pastels, earth tones) to create a safe and inviting atmosphere.
- **Low-Poly Charm:** Maintain a consistent low-poly 3D style (e.g., Quaternius-inspired) that is visually readable and performant on mobile/tablet devices.
- **Touch-First UI:** Interactive elements (buttons, radial menus) should be large, spaced generously, and responsive to touch gestures.
- **Minimalist HUD:** Keep the on-screen display clean. Use intuitive icons and soft animations rather than dense text to convey information like health or happiness.

## Tone & Voice
- **Positive & Encouraging:** The baby dino and any system messages should always speak with a friendly, gentle, and supportive tone.
- **Child-Friendly Language:** Avoid jargon. Use simple, evocative language that is easy for children to understand and engage with.
- **Empathetic Interactions:** Dino reactions should feel emotionally resonant—showing joy when petted and curiosity when exploring.

## UX Principles
- **Low-Friction Exploration:** Movement and interactions should feel fluid. Avoid steep learning curves or punishing mechanics.
- **Safe & Private:** Always prioritize user privacy. Clearly communicate when sensors (camera/mic) are active and ensure all processing is ephemeral.
- **Immediate Feedback:** Every touch action (e.g., tapping the ground, petting) should provide clear visual or haptic feedback so the player feels connected to the world.
- **Parental Peace of Mind:** Design for a calm experience that encourages positive emotional connection without overstimulation.

## Interaction Patterns
- **The Radial Menu:** The primary tool for dino care. It should feel tactile and "physical," appearing directly at the point of interaction.
- **Tap-to-Move:** The standard for exploration, ensuring the player can navigate the world intuitively.
- **Responsive AI:** The dino's responses to vision/audio should feel natural and timely, reinforcing the illusion of a living creature.
