# Initial Concept
A cozy, tablet-first, touch-only 3D companion “open world” sandbox featuring a baby dino driven by Google Gemini.

# Product Definition
**Tucker & Baby Dino** is a warm, interactive 3D companion RPG designed for touch-based devices. It blends low-poly aesthetics with the advanced multimodal intelligence of Google Gemini to create a unique creature-raising and exploration experience.

## Core Pillars
1. **Multimodal Companionship:** The baby dino sees (vision), hears (audio), and speaks (TTS) using Gemini Flash models, creating a reactive and personalized bond.
2. **Engaging Interactivity:** The primary focus is on the baby dino's "impressive" and "stimulating" presence, aimed at positively impacting a child's heart and mind.
3. **Cozy Exploration:** A peaceful open world with villages, streams, and forests, designed for low-stress interaction.
4. **Touch-First Accessibility:** Simple tap-to-move mechanics and a radial menu designed specifically for tablets.

## Target Audience
- Children and families seeking a gentle, creative companion.
- Tech-savvy users exploring the intersection of Generative AI and 3D gaming.

## Key Features
- **Multimodal AI Brain:** Real-time facial expression and voice chunk processing for the dino's "brain," personality, and emotional engagement.
- **Interactive Radial Menu:** Actions like Pet, Feed, Play, and Camp that stimulate the dino's growth and reactions.
- **Dynamic World:** Day/Night cycles synced to local time.
- **Local Progression:** Initial systems for hunger, cleanliness, and happiness that evolve over time and impact growth.

## Future Vision (Long-term)
- **Competitive RPG Progression:** Inspired by *Pokémon* and *Monster Rancher*, the dino grows through battles and training, evolving in appearance, style, and skills.
- **Strategic Evolution Tree:** A cross-disciplinary system where traits, moves, and physical attributes (e.g., color, stoutness, speed) evolve.
- **Shared Worlds (Multiplayer):** A system where players can join each other's worlds and interact with their uniquely evolved companions.

## Success Criteria
- A seamless loop of interacting with the dino, managing its needs, and exploring the world.
- A profound sense of companionship and positive emotional impact on the child.
- Low-latency, high-quality AI responses that feel "alive."
- A visually coherent and stable 3D environment that runs smoothly on tablet hardware.
